How Clan Wars Work


Each epic two days Clan War is won by the clan who scores the most stars.
Spend day one preparing troops, designing your base and scouting your enemies.
Day two is Battle Day, when you scores stars by attacking the enemy clan.

At end of the war bonus loot is delivered to your Clan Castle for each of yours successful attacks.
Winning also earns a large XP bonus for your Clan. Leveling up your Clan unlock awesome Clan War perks.
